The Cable TV News Dataset

The dataset queried using the Stanford Cable TV News Analyzer includes near 24/7 recordings of CNN, FOX News, and MSNBC from January 1, 2010 to July 23, 2019. In total, the dataset consists of over 230,000 hours of video and includes both TV news programming and commercial segments. All videos include audio and most non-commercial segments have accompanying closed caption transcripts. All source video and transcripts were provided by The Internet Archive's TV News Archive.
